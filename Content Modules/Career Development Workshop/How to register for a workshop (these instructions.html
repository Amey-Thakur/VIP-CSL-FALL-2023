1. Log on to mySuccess
  2. Click on Career on the left hand menu
  3. Click on Career Events on the left hand menu
  4. Click on Career Services Events & Workshops Overview still in the left hand menu and review the descriptions  (pick something that interests you and is relevant to your degree undergraduate or graduate)
  5. Click on View Career Calendar on the right and look for a workshop that suits your schedule and your interest
  6. Click on the event on the calendar
  7. Click on Register for this Event 

NOTE: If registration for the workshop you are interested in is not open yet, mark your calendar to go on mysuccess 10 days before the event for register.

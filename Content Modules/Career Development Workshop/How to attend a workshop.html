Once registered for a workshop:

  1. Put the workshop into your calendar and set a reminder for yourself
  2. Prepare any materials that the workshop description asks you to, at least one day in advance
  3. Arrive 5-10 minutes early to your workshop (check if it is on Teams or in-person in JEC 100)
  4. Ask questions and be engaged during the workshop
  5. After the workshop has ended, take the quiz to track your attendance (it's the last step in this module)

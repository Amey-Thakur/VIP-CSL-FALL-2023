Everyone must complete one Career workshop before the deadline given on the timeline.  We have variety of career workshops available so you should be able to find something that you are interested in. Register for a workshop in advance, and you will receive a reminder message 24 hours before. 

After you have attended answer the quiz to let VIP staff know you have completed this requirement. The quiz is available on the Quizzes tab above. You do not need to tell the workshop facilitator you are attending for VIP, they will automatically track your attendance as long as you arrived on time, stayed for the duration, and participated.

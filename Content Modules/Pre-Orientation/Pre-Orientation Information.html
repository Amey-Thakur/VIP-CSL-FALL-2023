Review three powerpoint presentations about this great experience and what will be expected of you. Also read the Timeline and Contract (in the left hand menu). You are responsible for learning all of the content contained in these resources. Study and then take the quiz! The quiz will have questions that relate to content in the presentations, the contract and the timeline. You must score at least 90% on the quiz. You will be given only three attempts on the quiz. There is feedback given with incorrect answers to help you. If you have any questions, contact vip@uwindsor.ca.

Complete the quiz before the deadline. If you do not score 90% within 3 attempts or you miss the deadline you will not be able to participate in the VIP program.

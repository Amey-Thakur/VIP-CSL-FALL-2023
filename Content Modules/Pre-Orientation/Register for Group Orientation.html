To register for orientation:

  1. Log into mySuccess
  2. Choose VIP-CSL on left hand menu
  3. Choose VIP Events
  4. Click on "View Calendar" on the upper right side 
  5. Go to the correct month
  6. Select one of the options for VIP-CSL Orientation (all sessions are in-person)
  7. Click on the "Register" button

Complete all the pre-orientation steps in this module, before the deadline given, so that your attendance will count! Arrive 5m early so we can track your attendance. Be ready to listen and ask questions!

If you are late, your attendance will not count. If you are late you will not be allowed to enter the room. If you are late to the last session, or do not attend a session, you will not be able to complete this experience.

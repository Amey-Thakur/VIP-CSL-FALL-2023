You may be asked to provide your immunization records for childhood vaccines and COVID-19, to be screened for TB, to get a flu shot, or to visit a doctor and have a health-related form completed. Each organization is a little different so carefully review the position description to look for this information and ask at your first meeting with your community partner supervisor. If you do not understand why you have to do something, please ask your supervisor and they will be happy to explain. It is your responsibility to complete these additional requirements as quickly as possible so you can begin your VIP placement.

NOTE: A two-step TB test can be done here on campus and it takes 2 weeks to complete so you should start it right away if you need it for your placement. Click here to learn more about what a two-step TB test is.
